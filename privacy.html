Privacy Policy

We built DopShield V2 to reduce tracking and minimize data while supporting lawful compliance requirements.

What we collect

  • Contact details you submit (like email) if you request access or support.
  • Verification data only if you enter a verified onboarding flow. We scope collection to what the compliance provider requires.
  • Operational logs with short retention. We avoid storing IP addresses long-term unless required for security incidents.

What we do not do

  • No selling personal data.
  • No third-party ad trackers.
  • No building cross-site profiles.

Encryption

Protected content is encrypted. Where end-to-end encryption is enabled, servers cannot read the payload.

Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for service delivery, security, and legal requirements. We aim for short retention windows by default.
