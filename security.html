Security

We design for privacy and safety at the same time. We do not market or build for illicit concealment.

Core controls

  • Passkeys supported. Optional 2FA.
  • Client-side encryption for protected payloads where enabled.
  • Short retention for metadata where feasible.
  • Rate limits, anomaly detection, and abuse response.
  • Approval-gated audit mode for scoped disclosures.

Reporting

If you find a vulnerability, email hello@example.com with steps to reproduce. Do not exfiltrate data.
